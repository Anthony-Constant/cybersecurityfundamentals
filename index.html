📘 Module 1: Cyber Security Fundamentals: Part 1

With cybercrime on the rise, organizations must take proactive measures to protect their systems and data. Ethical hacking — also known as penetration testing — is a powerful approach used to identify vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them.

In this module, we’ll cover foundational cybersecurity concepts including common hacker profiles, penetration testing types and methodologies, and how the CIA Triad helps organizations structure their security strategy.

  • Understand the role of ethical hackers vs. black hats and script kiddies
  • Explore white, black, and grey box testing strategies
  • Get introduced to red team vs. blue team simulations
  • Learn the CIA Triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability
